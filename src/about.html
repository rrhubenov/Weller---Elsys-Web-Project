Every person has the ability to feel empathy and give support to others!
Weller is a site which anonymously connects you to a person from somewhere around the world
and allows you to freely express your problems and concerns with another human being!
If you feel like the person with which you connected has helped you, you can always donate directly to him!
Donating will help the person in his own problems and you will return the favor!
